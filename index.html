Lxcent

Commissioned Avatar Polish and Set-up for VRChat

Heya, I’m Lxcent!

I specialize in avatar rigging for VRChat, focusing on setting up Models for a Polished Set up for VRchat! I don't do Modelling or Texturing, nor anything that has to do with Blender, including Bone creation and Weight Painting. I do however, do Rigging, Expression Support, and Polishing of existing Models. I also do Avatar Adjustments for existing Models that need some fixes or adjustments to be VRChat ready.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Important Note: Commissions for Quest/Mobile avatars are currently Unavailable for Prices and under certain circumstances are free due to the prioritization of PC Avatars in my Queue. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
