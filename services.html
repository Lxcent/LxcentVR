What I offer for avatar commissions.

These are the systems and setup features I can help with for VRChat avatars.

Contacts

Setup and adjustment of contact systems for interactive avatar behavior.

Colliders

Collider set-ups for improved interaction between Body and Physbones such as Hair.

Physbones

Custom physics-driven movement for a more lively and polished result, avoiding clipping issues for most cases.

Toggles

Toggle-based features for accessories, effects, or avatar states.

Expression Menu

Structure and organization for expression menus and avatar controls.

Emissives

Material and shader-based emissive effects for extra visual flair.

Texture Initialization

Setup and cleanup for texture-based elements so they work properly.

Audio Link

Integration support for responsive audio-driven effects and visual reactions.

Basic Unity Animations

Simple animation implementation for avatar actions and presentation.

GoGoLoco

Support for locomotion-style avatar movement systems and setup adjustments.

Custom Hand Gestures

Custom hand gesture implementations for a more expressive avatar experience.

What I do not offer

I keep my work focused on setup and polish rather than full creation from scratch.

Model Creations

I do not create full character models from scratch.

Bone Creation

I do not build brand-new skeletons or rig foundations from zero.

Weight Painting

I do not perform detailed weight-painting work for full model builds.

Clothing Creation

I do not create clothing meshes or fashion assets for avatars.

Full Blender Animations

I do not produce large-scale Blender animation projects.

Blender Related Things

I keep my focus on VRChat-related setup and support, keeping my Services only Unity related rather than broad Blender work.
