Commission terms

Clear expectations for every commission.

These terms help keep the process smooth, fair, and straightforward.

Free Avatar Inspection

Every Commission includes a brief review of your avatar to ensure i can apply my Services in the first place.

Asset Ownership

By commissioning my services, the client agrees that the Avatar as well as Provided assets belong to them or they have the right to use and change them.

Privacy

I will keep your project details and personal information private and respectful. After completion of the Commission, all information will be securely deleted unless otherwise agreed.

Payment

If services can be applied, the payment will be split into 2 parts: a deposit to initiate the work and the remaining balance upon completion.

Delivery

Completed work is only delivered once the payment is received.

Revisions

Minor revisions are included when possible, while larger changes may require extra time or cost.

Scope Changes

Additional requests outside the agreed scope may be handled as separate work.

Communication

Clear communication helps the commission stay smooth and the client must provide all of the files to work with unless it is one of my Services to create assets.

Completion Time

Estimated completion time depends on workload, complexity, and current queue.

Right to Decline

I may decline work that does not fit my skills, are offensive or illegal, are ripped or violate creator licenses.

Supported Software

I mainly work within Unity and the associated tools for avatar setup and rigging.

Refund Policy

Refunds are handled case by case. Full refund before work, partial refund during work, no refund after completion.

Work in Progress Updates

I will share progress updates where relevant so you know how the project is moving along. Especially during larger scale commissions.

No Hidden Costs

Any expected costs will be discussed clearly before work begins.

Respectful Conduct

Respectful and professional communication is expected throughout the commission process. If the communication becomes abusive, the commission may be terminated by me or the client. Both parties reserve the right to terminate the commission under such circumstances.

Transparency in Skills

I will be clear about what I can do well and where I may need to limit the scope.

Quality Before Delivery

I aim to deliver work that is polished and tested before it is handed over.

NSFW

I do not take on NSFW-related commissions unless clearly discussed and agreed upon.

Quality Expectations

Quality may vary depending on the complexity of the request such as VRChat Avatar Limits and Expressiveness. For higher expressiveness, it is expected that the Avatars Quality will be poor, while Optimized Avatars will have less Expressiveness. On top of that the Quality may not reach perfection due to Technicalities, constraints and expressiveness.
